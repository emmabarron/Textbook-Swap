About

SMRITI is a fast and simple way to buy and sell textbooks. To buy a textbook, simply put in the ISBN of the book you're looking for. You'll then be given a list of books uploaded to SMRITI, as well as listings of used books from eBay and Amazon. If you're selling, simply give us some information about your textbook, and you'll be up on our website! You can see your active entries and what you've sold on your profile page. There, you can mark what items you've sold, or remove listings if you'd like.


The word "smriti" comes from Sanskrit, roughly meaning "knowledge imparted through writing." SMRITI's mission is to help you gain this knowledge, by making the process of finding and selling textbooks seamless.
